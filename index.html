Car Mileage

Examining the effects of various aspects of a car on its mileage through an interactive slideshow. Data from the https://flunky.github.io/cars2017.csv dataset.

Use your arrow keys or click the arrows on your screen to move between slides.

How do various car makes differ in mileage per gallon?

Different car brands are known to be more or less efficient in terms of mileage.

Use the toggle button to switch between average city MPG and average highway MPG. Hover over bars to see the specific make.

How do fuel types differ in their highway mileage per gallon?

Different fuels have distinct energy efficiencies and combustion characteristics that impact their mileage.

Hover over a specific fuel type to view the average highway MPG.
